Since others are NOT on your WiFi, deploy online!

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ EASIEST WAY: VERCEL (2 Minutes)                                          │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Double-click: deploy-to-vercel.bat

2. When browser opens:
   - Sign up at vercel.com (free, use GitHub or email)
   - Authorize Vercel

3. In the terminal:
   - Press Enter to confirm settings
   - Wait for deployment

4. Copy the URL you get (e.g., https://your-site.vercel.app)

5. Share that URL with anyone, anywhere!

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ALTERNATIVE: NETLIFY (Drag & Drop)                                       │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Double-click: deploy-to-netlify.bat

2. Sign up at netlify.com (free)

3. Drag the "dist" folder to Netlify

4. Get URL instantly!
